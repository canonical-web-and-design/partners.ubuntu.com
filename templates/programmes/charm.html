Charm Partner Programme

If you’re an ISV or solution provider focused on the cloud or big data, you know how difficult it can be for your customers to realise the full value of your software. Releasing your solutions with Juju, the award-winning service orchestration tool from Canonical, can help your customers use your product at every stage; from the initial proof of concept, to development, through to full scale deployment.

Deploy your software the right way

Juju automates and speeds up the deployment, scaling and managing of distributed applications. By creating a Juju charm for your product, you make it easy for administrators and devops teams to integrate it with hundreds of other solutions.

It gets better. Juju charms can be combined in bundles: if your software is usually deployed with other services, a customer can use a charm bundle to deploy all the services together. All the detailed configuration settings and relationships between services are handled by Juju.

What’s included?

As a partner involved in the Charm Partner Programme you can expect:

  • Inclusion in our online charm database, available for instant download
  • QA to ensure your charms meets the highest quality standards
  • Training and guidance on writing charms for your products
  • Co-marketing opportunities including joint events, videos, blogs, PR and demos to potential customers

Benefits

  • Increased revenue opportunities. Juju charms and bundles can be deployed on a huge range of platforms and servers including: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, IBM Cloud, Joyent, bare metal x86, IBM POWER8 and ARM servers.
  • More efficient sales cycle. Juju charms make it easy for potential clients to spin up a proof of concept and start evaluating your products instantly.
  • Enable customer agility. Best practice is built into the charm, resulting in higher quality deployments and happier customers every time.
  • Be part of the latest technology trends. Charms and bundles make it easy to get your products included in today’s technologies (e.g. cloud, scale-out, Big Data, Docker, etc.)
  • Reduce costs because charms are reusable, easy to deploy, and provide immediate integration with other charmed applications.
  • Create better customer experiences. Juju charms simplifies big, distributed systems.
