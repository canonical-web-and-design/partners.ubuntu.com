Deploy, connect and scale

Juju automates and speeds up the deployment, scaling and managing of distributed applications. By creating a Juju charm for your product, you make it easy for administrators and devops teams to integrate it with hundreds of other solutions.

It gets better. Juju charms can be combined in bundles: if your software is usually deployed with other services, a customer can use a charm bundle to deploy all the services together. All the detailed configuration settings and relationships between services are handled by Juju.

What’s included?

As a partner involved in the Charm Partner Programme you can expect:

  • Inclusion in our online charm database, available for instant deployment
  • QA to ensure your charms meets the highest quality standards
  • Training and guidance on writing charms for your products
  • Co-marketing opportunities such as joint events, videos, blogs, PR and demos to potential customers

Benefits

  • Optimise sales cycle by making it easy for potential clients to use Juju to spin up a proof of concept and start evaluating your products instantly.
  • Best practice is built into the charm, resulting in higher quality deployments and happier customers every time.
  • Increase your revenue opportunities as Juju charms and bundles can be deployed on a huge range of platforms and servers including; AWS, Azure, Google Cloud and on bare metal; x86, IBM POWER8 and ARM servers.
  • Charms and bundles make it easy to get your products easily integrated into today’s hottest technologies (e.g. cloud, scale-out, Big Data, Docker, etc.)
  • Create better customer experiences as Juju charms simplify big, distributed systems.
  • Charms help you reduce costs as they are reusable, easy to deploy, and provide immediate integration with other charmed applications.
